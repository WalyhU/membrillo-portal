# Membrillo Portal Design System

This design system is inspired by the artisanal tradition of "El Membrillo" (quince jellies). 

## Principles
- **Heritage & Trust:** Use deep reds and gold to evoke tradition and quality.
- **Clarity:** Information (especially stock levels) should be highly legible.
- **Modern Simplicity:** Clean lines and ample whitespace to balance the traditional color palette.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Deep red background with white text. Slightly rounded corners.
- **Secondary:** Gold background for highlighting stock or secondary actions.

### Cards
- Elevation 1 by default, Elevation 2 on hover.
- Product images should have a soft cream background.
